Cardiology

The Cardiology Department at HealthUp provides comprehensive heart care with expert consultations, advanced diagnostics, and personalized treatment plans.

Our specialists help manage conditions like hypertension, arrhythmias, and heart disease with remote monitoring and personalized advice. With advanced technology and a patient-first approach.

View More

Neurology

The Neurology Department at HealthUp provides expert care for brain, nerve, and spinal disorders through online consultations and advanced diagnostics.

Our specialists treat conditions like migraines, epilepsy, stroke, and neurological disorders with personalized care plans. With remote monitoring and expert guidance, we ensure timely diagnosis and effective treatment to improve brain health and overall well-being.

View More

General Medicine

Comprehensive primary care and internal medicine for everyday health issues.

General Medicine focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of common health issues like infections, fever, diabetes, and hypertension. It provides primary care and helps manage overall wellness with personalized medical advice and preventive care.

View More

Pediatrics

The Pediatrics Department at HealthUp offers expert healthcare for infants, children, and adolescents through online consultations and remote monitoring.

Our pediatricians provide treatment for common childhood illnesses, growth and development concerns, vaccinations, and nutrition guidance. With a child-friendly approach, we ensure comprehensive and compassionate care for your little ones.

View More

Eye Care

The Eye Care Department at HealthUp provides expert diagnosis and treatment for vision and eye health issues through online consultations.

Our specialists offer care for conditions like dry eyes, infections, refractive errors, and early detection of cataracts or glaucoma. With personalized guidance and remote eye health assessments, we help you maintain clear and healthy vision.
